About Us

Fresho is Sweden’s best online grocery store, based in Stockholm and delivering nationwide. We offer a wide selection of groceries, snacks, and fresh vegetables—sourced directly from farmers and delivered to your doorstep. Each product we offer is a link in a chain that connects Mother Nature, our dedicated farmers, and you. At Fresho, we believe in fresh food, fast delivery, and a more connected way to shop.
